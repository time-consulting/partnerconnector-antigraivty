A) “Show me around” (pop-up pill tour)

Format: small pill bottom-left; 1 line + “Next / Skip”.
Timing: only on first login; ~5 pills; < 30s total.

Welcome pill
“Welcome! 20-second tour of your new hub.” → Next

Dashboard
“Your pipeline at a glance. Update deals fast.” → Next

Add Referral
“Submit a new lead in under a minute.” → Next

Earnings
“Track commissions + tiers. Silver is 5 wins/month.” → Next

Invite & Overrides
“Invite teammates—earn up to 3 levels down.” → Finish

Dismiss rule: If skipped, show a tiny “Help → Quick tour” link in the header.

B) Quick setup (60-second essentials)

UI: single card, 2 columns desktop / stacked on mobile.
Copy: short hints; everything save-on-blur. A single Save & continue.

Fields (minimal, useful):

First name (required)
Hint: “For payouts & updates.”

Last name (required)

Phone (required)
Hint: “We’ll text status updates.”

Company name (optional)
Checkbox: “No company yet”

Role (chips): Accountant • Consultant • Advisor • Other

Country (dropdown) – defaults from IP

(Auto-filled & hidden): Email from auth

After submit (toast):
“Nice—profile set! +25 XP”

Defer the heavy stuff:
Show a slim banner: “Add payout details to get paid faster.” → “Add now” / “Later”

C) Early invite nudge (3-level earning)

Goal: plant the seed immediately, keep it light.

Banner (after profile save):
Title: “Earn more with your team”
Line: “Invite partners and earn overrides up to 3 levels.”
CTAs: Invite now • Copy link
Fine print link: “How 3-level earnings work”

Tap-through explainer (10s max):

“You earn on your deals.”

“You also earn a % of theirs.”

“And a smaller % of their recruits.”
CTA: Get my invite link

Share modal (pre-filled):
“Hey! I’m using PartnerConnector to share client leads for payments/funding. Join my team—earn commissions, and we both benefit. Sign up here: {invite_link}”

Gamification nudges:

Toast after first invite: “Invite sent! +10 XP”

Right-rail card: “2 invites = Starter badge (+20 XP)”

Microcopy pack (paste-ready)

Tour skip: “Skip tour”

Save button: “Save & continue”

Phone hint: “We’ll text status updates.”

Company checkbox: “No company yet”

Payout banner: “Add payout details to get paid faster.”

Invite banner: “Invite partners—earn 3 levels down.”

Copy link btn: “Copy link” (toast: “Copied!”)

Explainer link: “How 3-level earnings work”

UX guardrails

Keep each pill under 9 words.

Never exceed 6 fields on the setup card.

Everything is optional except First name, Last name, Phone.

Always offer Later on anything money/KYC.

Autosave on blur; show small “Saved ✓”.

Show progress ring only if it helps (e.g., “Profile 80%”).

Instrumentation (what to track)

tour_started, tour_completed

profile_submitted (fields completeness score)

invite_banner_viewed, invite_link_copied, invite_sent

Time-to-first-referral (T1R)
